Headline:
Don't have scheduling software? Ours thinks for you.

Body:
Backfill Shifts isn't just a scheduler. It's an AI-native scheduling layer built directly into your Backfill account — one that builds your schedule, learns your operation, and updates itself in plain language.

Tell it what you need. "Add a closing shift Friday, same crew as last week." Done. "Daniela can't do Tuesday — move her to Thursday." Done. No grid-clicking. No manual juggling. Just say what you need and your schedule reflects it.

When a shift opens, the Backfill coverage engine takes over automatically — same as any integrated scheduler, without the third-party subscription.

Note: Included for all Backfill customers. No additional cost.

Features list (right column):

AI schedule generation
Describe your week, your roles, your team. Backfill Shifts drafts the schedule. You approve, adjust, or just say what's wrong.

Natural language edits
No forms, no dropdowns. Type or say the change, and the schedule updates. It's as fast as sending a text.

Pattern learning
The more you use it, the better it knows your operation. Recurring roles, preferred staff, shift windows — it stops asking what you always do.

Real-time coverage board
Every open shift, every in-progress fill attempt, every confirmation — across all locations — at a glance.

Standby queue management
Primary fill falls through? Your pre-ranked standby queue activates automatically. No second round of calls from you.

Upgrade anytime
Grow into a dedicated platform later? Everything ports over. No rebuilding from scratch.

Section label: FAQ

Headline:
Questions operators actually ask.

Subheadline:
Straight answers. No runaround.

Layout: Accordion (one open at a time)

Q: How does Backfill know who to call?
A: During setup, you define your team, their roles, and their availability. Backfill uses that data to build a prioritized call list — by role fit, availability, and standing. You control the order. We work it.

Q: What happens if nobody picks up or says yes?
A: Backfill keeps working the list. If the shift goes unfilled, you're notified immediately so you can step in — but you're never left in the dark mid-attempt. And you don't pay for unfilled shifts.

Q: Do my employees need to download an app?
A: No. Backfill reaches them the way they already communicate — by phone. No app installs, no new logins, no behavior change required from your team.

Q: What if I already use a scheduling platform?
A: Backfill integrates directly with 7shifts, Deputy, When I Work, and Homebase. Your existing schedule syncs automatically — no duplicate setup.

Q: What is Backfill Shifts, and do I have to use it?
A: Backfill Shifts is our built-in AI-native scheduling layer for businesses that don't have a scheduling platform. It's included with your Backfill account at no extra cost. If you already have a scheduler, you don't need it — but it's there if you do.

Q: How does billing work?
A: You pay $20 per successfully filled shift, plus a one-time location setup fee. If a shift goes unfilled, you're not charged. No monthly fees, no minimums, no surprises.

Q: How long does setup take?
A: Most customers are live within 24 hours of their first call with us. That's it.

Q: Is Backfill only for restaurants?
A: Backfill was built with restaurant and hospitality operators in mind — environments where shift coverage is mission-critical and time-sensitive. If that sounds like your operation, we're a fit.
