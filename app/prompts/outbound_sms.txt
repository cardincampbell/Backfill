Outbound SMS templates for Tier 1/2 outreach.
These are sent by the system (not spoken by the voice agent) via Twilio.

---
INITIAL OFFER (send first):

Hi [worker_name], this is Backfill reaching out for [restaurant_name].
Open shift: [role] on [date], [start_time]–[end_time] @ $[pay_rate]/hr.
Reply YES to take it or NO to pass. Questions? Call 1-800-BACKFILL.

---
CONFIRMATION (after YES reply):

You're confirmed! [role] at [restaurant_name] on [date] at [start_time].
Address: [address]. Any questions, call 1-800-BACKFILL. See you there!

---
DECLINE ACKNOWLEDGMENT (after NO reply):

No worries! We'll find someone else. Thanks for the quick reply.
– Backfill

---
REMINDER (30 min before shift if no response):

Reminder from Backfill: The [role] shift at [restaurant_name] starts at [start_time] today.
You're confirmed — see you there! Questions? Call 1-800-BACKFILL.

---
OPT-OUT CONFIRMATION (after STOP):

You've been unsubscribed from Backfill shift texts. Reply START anytime to re-subscribe.
– Backfill (1-800-BACKFILL)

---
NOTE: All messages must include opt-out language on first contact per TCPA requirements.
Never send to workers with sms_consent_status != 'granted'.
